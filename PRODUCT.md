# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Socialize serves developers, open-source maintainers, technical writers, and independent builders who need one durable URL for their work. They use the hosted editor when they want to publish quickly, or the self-hosted edition when they want to own the deployment, Firebase project, domain, and data path.

## Product Purpose

Socialize turns a developer's projects, writing, social accounts, and availability into a focused public profile. Success means a user can claim a handle, build a credible page without editing code, publish it, and later export the same portable profile shape for self-hosting without rebuilding their identity from scratch.

## Brand Personality

Direct, technical, independent. The product should feel confident and carefully made without becoming corporate, loud, or cute. Marketing can be expressive and cinematic; authenticated tools should become quieter and keep attention on the user's work.

## Anti-references

- Generic creator-economy link pages filled with promotional clutter, engagement bait, and platform lock-in.
- Template-heavy SaaS dashboards where every idea becomes a rounded card, gradient, or decorative metric.
- Developer tools that equate technical credibility with illegible terminal cosplay or excessive neon effects.
- Motion that delays access to forms, hides content, or ignores reduced-motion preferences.

## Design Principles

- Put the developer's work before Socialize branding.
- Make ownership visible through export, self-hosting, and a shared data shape.
- Keep editing and previewing close enough that every change is understandable.
- Use privacy choices and system status language that says exactly what happens.
- Let marketing carry personality while product controls stay familiar and efficient.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for color contrast, keyboard access, focus visibility, form labels, error messaging, and screen-reader semantics. Respect reduced-motion preferences, never use color as the only state cue, keep touch targets usable on mobile, and preserve readable text at browser zoom and narrow widths.
